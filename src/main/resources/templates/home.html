O nama

Natura Resort is a boutique hotel mixing design, comfort and nature which make the place unique in Siem Reap and the concept original in the Kingdom. Located on the riverside road going to the temples and 5 minutes only from town, the hotel is easy to find and to access. Situated in a Cambodian authentic area, the resort is 100 meters away from a pagoda and ruins temple, in the middle of the Angkorian forest.
